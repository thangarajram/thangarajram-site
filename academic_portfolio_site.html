About

I am a former supply-chain management consultant, currently conducting human-computer interaction research on what happens to our data after we die. I learnt how to design ships during my bachelor’s in Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering in IIT Madras (India), got into design during my master’s in User Experience in BCU (UK), and am currently getting curiosity quenched by my PhD research at NJIT (USA). I also write poetry, and play a few musical instruments (drums, keyboard, and the ukulele).
